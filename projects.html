~Croissants~

Chocolate
Croissants

A chocolate croissant, also
known as a "pain au
chocolat," is a type of pastry
that is made from layers of
buttery, flaky dough shaped
into a rectangle and filled
with chocolate.

Croissant Burger

A croissant burger is a
creative twist on the
traditional burger, replacing
the standard bun with a
flaky, buttery croissant for
a rich, decadent, and
unique dining experience.

Butter Croissant

A croissant is a buttery,
flaky pastry, named for its
crescent shape, made from a
laminated dough of thin
layers of butter and dough.
The name refers to the
crescent shape of the pastry
and is pronounced roughly
like "quah-sah".

Croissant
Sandwich

A croissant sandwich
elevates a simple meal
by pairing its buttery,
flaky pastry with savory
fillings like smoked salmon,
cheese, and eggs, or even
sweet and savory combos.
